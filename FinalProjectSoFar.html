Progress So Far

At the current state of work I have done research into various topics that are connected to my interests in energy, behavioural nudges and how people perceive planetary problems. With this initial research conducted Oliver and I set out to better understand our personal behavior and how various tools impact our usage. We learned quite quickly that if the tool isn't streamlined it will not function for the majority of the people. Even us who were interested in the topic couldn't keep up with using our personal tracker.

From here we went on to reach out to people within our community to create a broader understanding of what different people and designers might feel about our project. We started by sending out a general survey to people in the Netherlands, UK and Spain, to find out what they felt about energy, tracking and AI. From here we hosted a collaborative design session with 8 other MDEF students to get insights into our problems,

In tandem to the research we did, we spent the various micro challenges testing and developing an idea that is about how can we connect to ones energy at a building level and stimulate a learning process. We worked over various weeks to create both an open-sourced low-cost energy monitor, and our energy bot Dan. Currently both of these elements are up and running, but there is still a lot of development left. To learn more about the state of the system and how it works check out the following Github Repo.
